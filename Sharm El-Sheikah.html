Sharm El-Sheikah

Sharm El Sheikh is a unique place because it is a premier international resort destination that offers an unparalleled combination of luxurious resorts and one of the world's most spectacular natural marine environments. Nicknamed the "City of Peace" for the many international peace conferences it has hosted, its primary allure lies beneath the waves of the Red Sea, which is renowned for having some of the healthiest, most vibrant coral reefs and clearest waters on Earth.
